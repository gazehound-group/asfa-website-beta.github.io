Trial Date Request Form

Use this form to request, change, or cancel a trial date.

ATTENTION:

When requesting a trial date, please use this on-line date request form. Please use the SCHEDULING@ASFA.ORG address for all Scheduling issues.

Required Information & Rules

Location: Trial site and nearest major city.
Trial Type: All-Breed / Specialty / Invitational / Fun Trial.
(To schedule a Regional Invitational, a club must receive written approval from its Regional Director).
Priority: Check the box if the requested date is intended as a priority date for the following year.
Limits: Max 8 trial dates (up to 4 weekends) at any one time. A club can hold priority on a max of 6 dates (up to 3 weekends). Held until 120 days prior.
