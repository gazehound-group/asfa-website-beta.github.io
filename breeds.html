ASFA Eligible Breeds

Steeped in the traditions, politics and intrigues of the royal courts of the ancient world, sighthounds have always been admired by royalty for their beauty, speed, endurance, agility, efficiency, and dignity.

These breeds are among the oldest known dog breeds, many changed little in form from the times of the Pharaohs, Tsars, Lairds, Sheiks, Kings and Queens of Europe, Africa and Asia. Owning a sighthound is like owning a small piece of that history.

PROVISIONAL BREEDS are eligible to run in Open, Field Champion and Veteran Stakes. Points and placements are awarded but no title certificates are issued until the breed is fully recognized by the ASFA. These hounds are NOT eligible for the Best in Field or Best in Event runs.
