Key to Trial Result Codes

Stake (#, 2NQ, Dis/DQ): The first number indicates the number of hounds competing for points and determines the number of points awarded in that stake. The numbers following indicate hounds which are non-qualifying, dismissed/disqualified.

NC Absent, pulled, or excused (0 pts)
B Best of Breed
R Runoff
T Tie
E Excused
F Runoff Forfeited
*(hound name)* = Hound is ineligible to be entered in that stake. Points/placements remain the same for all other hounds in that stake.
