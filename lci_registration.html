Request for ASFA Lure Chasing Instinct Registration Number

Attention:

This form is ONLY for dogs without any registration number from another registering body (such as AKC, UKC, PAL, FSS, Canine Partners, etc.). If your dog already has a registration number from an approved registry, you do not need to fill out this form.

FEE & INSPECTION PROCESS:

A one-time registration fee of $10.00 must be submitted to the Field Trial Secretary (FTS) at your first trial. The FTS or an official inspector will measure (wicket) your dog at inspection to determine its size classification (Small or Large). After inspection, the FTS will provide you with a receipt, and the ASFA Records Coordinator will assign your official LCI Registration Number.
