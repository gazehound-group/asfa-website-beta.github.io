Apprentice Judge Application

Submit this application to request a provisional license to judge ASFA sanctioned lure field trials.

Requirements Overview:

• You must be at least 18 years old and in good standing with ASFA.

• You must have participated in lure coursing for a minimum of 3 consecutive years.

• You must have served in an official capacity at a minimum of 10 ASFA trials.

• A $30.00 application fee is required upon submission.
